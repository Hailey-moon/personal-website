Making A New Social Media App

As I took part of Major League Hacking INIT 2022, one of the challenges was to make a new social media. I took on the challenge with the global pandemic in mind. This is an app to share sites for food and entertaintment near one's location through friends or through the "explore" page. Its purpose is to share new or lesser-known local spots and promote business in this post-pandemic world.
